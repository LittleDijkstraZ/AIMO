If he works 8 hours a day for 6 days a week, how much does he earn in a month?

## Solution
The boy works 8 hours a day for 6 days a week, so he works a total of 8 * 6 = 48 hours per week.
He earns $6 per hour, so he earns 48 * $6 = $288 per week.
Since he gets 2 weeks' pay cheque at the end of each month, he earns a total of 2 * $288 = $576 per month.
He has to give $20 per month for his meals, so his net income in a month is $576 - $20 = $556.
So the answer is $\boxed{556}$.

3x + 3 = 9

3x + 3 - 3 = 9 - 3

3x / 3 = 6 / 3
x = 2

So the solution to the equation 5x + 3 = 2x + 9 is x = 2.
